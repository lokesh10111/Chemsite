Research

A research brief is a concise summary or outline of a research project or study. It typically includes information such as the research question, objectives, methodology, data sources, analysis techniques, and expected outcomes. A research brief is often used as a preliminary document to communicate the key aspects of a research project to stakeholders such as funders, clients, or research teams. It can also serve as a guide for researchers to ensure that they stay focused and on track throughout the research process. The content and format of a research brief can vary depending on the specific requirements and audience, but it should always provide a clear and accurate description of the proposed research.
